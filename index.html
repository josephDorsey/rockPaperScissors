Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Here are the rules:

You and the computer will both pick between rock, paper, or scissors. The computers decision is randomly chosen. There will be 5 rounds.

  • Rock wins over scissors (because rock smashes scissors)
  • Scissors wins over paper (because scissors cut paper)
  • Paper wins over rock (because paper covers rock)
